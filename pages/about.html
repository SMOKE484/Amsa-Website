The Visionary Behind Alusani

Meet Masase Hangwani, the passionate educator changing lives through mathematics and science

"Education is not just about passing exams; it's about empowering young minds to transform their communities."

2015

The Vision Takes Root

After witnessing the struggles students faced with mathematics and science, Masase began tutoring students in his community, laying the foundation for what would become Alusani Academy.

2017

Establishment of Alusani

With just 12 students in a rented classroom, Alusani Academy officially opened its doors in Mashamba Village, focusing on personalized attention and concept mastery.

2020

Expansion and Recognition

The academy gained recognition for its exceptional results, with a 98% pass rate in mathematics and science.

2023

Community Impact

Alusani Academy now serves over 300 students annually and has become a model for community-based educational initiatives throughout the region.

Our Teaching Philosophy

The principles that guide our approach to education

Student-Centered Learning

We believe every student has unique learning needs. Our approach focuses on identifying individual strengths and addressing specific challenges through personalized attention.

Conceptual Understanding

Rather than memorization, we emphasize deep conceptual understanding. Students learn not just how to solve problems, but why solutions work, building a strong foundation for advanced learning.

Growth Mindset

We cultivate a growth mindset where challenges are viewed as opportunities. Mistakes are celebrated as valuable learning experiences on the path to mastery.
